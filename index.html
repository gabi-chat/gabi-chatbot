Frequently Asked Questions

  • What can I say to Gabi?
    Try “Hello”, “Play music”, “I feel anxious”, "dance", "get up", "I am hungry", "paper", "pencil", "write", "homework", "My name is...", "What's my name?", "How am I feeling?", "What's the weather today?"

  • What can Gabi do?
    Gabi can talk with you, remember your name and mood, play calming ambient sounds, respond to voice input, and even tell you the current weather.

  • Can Gabi remember things?
    Yes! Gabi can remember your name, your mood, and more. Try saying “My name is Alex” or “I feel happy”. Then ask “What’s my name?” or “How am I feeling?” later.

  • Can Gabi tell me the weather?
    Yes! Ask “What’s the weather like today?” and Gabi will give you the local forecast.

  • Can Gabi talk back?
    Yes! She uses voice output to respond gently.

  • How do I stop sounds?
    Say or type “Stop”, “Quiet”, or “Silence”.

  • Can I use my voice?
    Yes! Click the 🎤 button and speak to Gabi.

  • Does Gabi work offline?
    No, Gabi needs a modern browser like Chrome and an internet connection.
